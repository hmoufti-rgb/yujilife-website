Our mission

We believe a community thrives when every living thing within it is cared for.

Yujilife is built on a simple conviction: the challenges facing our environment, our animals, and our most vulnerable people are deeply connected — and so are their solutions.

We bring together volunteers, families, schools, local authorities and international partners to create lasting change on the ground, guided by a rights-based and inclusive approach aligned with the UN Sustainable Development Goals.

What we do

Four programs, working together.

01 — Environment

Protecting & restoring nature

Promoting and implementing initiatives that protect and restore the natural ecosystems we all depend on.

  • Tree-planting, waste reduction and sustainable everyday practices
  • Raising awareness on stewardship and engaging communities
  • Partnering with local authorities, schools and businesses

02 — Animal Protection

Rescue, shelter & welfare

Safeguarding animals through rescue, shelter, rehabilitation and adoption — and advocating for their rights.

  • Rescue, rehabilitation and adoption programmes
  • Mobilising volunteers for medical care, food and shelter
  • Events and campaigns advancing animal welfare policy

03 — Empowering Women

Independence & leadership

Enabling women's economic independence, leadership and full participation in community life.

  • Training, mentoring and capacity-building
  • Support for entrepreneurship and decision-making
  • Promoting gender equality and access to education

04 — Children with Special Needs

An inclusive training centre

An inclusive centre offering education, therapy and life skills for children with special needs — and their families.

  • Safe, adaptive learning environment for all
  • Education, rehabilitation, therapy and psychosocial support
  • Vocational and life-skills pathways

In focus · The Training Centre

Built for every child, supporting every family.

Our inclusive training centre gives children with special needs a safe, adaptive and supportive place to learn, grow and belong — with their families as active partners every step of the way.

Education & therapy

Inclusive, evidence-based curricula alongside speech, occupational, behavioural and sensory development support.

Life & vocational skills

Programmes for adolescents and young adults that open pathways to employability and economic inclusion.

Family-centered care

Parental guidance, counseling and workshops that empower families in their child's development.

Accessibility & innovation

Full physical, educational and digital accessibility — including assistive and AI-supported learning tools.
